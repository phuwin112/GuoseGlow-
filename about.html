Brand origin

毛戈平MAOGEPING

   Mr. MAO Geping draws inspiration from traditional Chinese ink wash painting, Western sketching and other art fields. With his decades of makeup experience, he combines makeup techniques from different fields to form a unique light and shadow makeup technique and light and shadow aesthetic system, fully demonstrating the unique charm of Eastern women.

   The brand has always been committed to researching and exploring beauty, constantly challenging itself. On the road to beauty, MAOGEPI NG is always by your side, inspiring your beauty. On the journey of life, MAOG EPING will accompany all women to face the challenges of this world with the most beautiful posture.

   Founded in 2017, Perfect Diary is committed to developing a range of high-quality, creative and amazing products, and intends to enable everyone to express and pursues their own beauty effortlessly. Meanwhile, Perfect Diary believes that the world will be a better place when any aspiration for beauty is valued.

PERFECT DIARY Founder

卡姿兰CARSLAN

   Specializing in high-quality makeup for 24 years

   Since its establishment in 2001, CARSLAN has adhered to the original aspiration of "enabling Chinese consumers to use the world's best makeup products", taking "technological innovation" as the core driving force, and has independently built a world-class makeup research and development center.

   Committed to providing consumers with professional, scientific and high-quality makeup products and makeup experiences, we constantly refine our consumer reputation and have been highly recognized by the industry and the market. In July 2025, we were awarded the title of "No. 1 Domestic Makeup Brand".

CARSLAN Founder

珀莱雅PROYA

 PROYA was born in Hangzhou in 2003. It is one of the earlier cosmetic brands launched by PROYA Cosmetics Co., Ltd. and focuses on deep-sea skin care research. With a distinct brand image, precise market positioning, and innovative marketing strategies, it has maintained a stable and rapid growth trend.

   PROYA takes technology as its foundation and, with a youthful and fearless spirit of discovery, is committed to providing scientific, safe and effective skin solutions, leading the aesthetic of skin technology.

   PROYA is committed to addressing the multi-faceted causes of skin problems that a single ingredient cannot cover, and creating a new skincare concept for the brand: "Scientific formula, the scientific choice for the skin."

PROYA Founder

彩棠TIMAGE

   TIMAGE is a makeup product created by Chinese celebrity makeup master Tang Yi based on Chinese aesthetics. With the brand concept of "Craftsmanship customization, simplifying complexity, and harmony between makeup and person", it combines practicality and artistry, helping every woman explore her own unique makeup style based on her own characteristics and showcase her distinctive beauty.

   TIMAGE was born in 2014, aiming to carry forward Chinese aesthetics from a modern perspective. With the concept of custom makeup, it helps every woman showcase her vitality and radiance. Mr. Tang Yi drew inspiration from Chinese ink-wash painting to create the "blank space" style of Chinese makeup techniques. With simple yet not simplistic makeup looks, it fully showcases the charm of the East and highlights the dynamic beauty of women.

TIMAGE Founder

韩束KANS

   KANS is a scientific anti-aging skincare brand founded in 2003, dedicated to researching the beauty of Asian skin. Backed by dual research laboratories in China and overseas and a high-quality supply chain system, it shapes product power through scientific research and is committed to providing young consumers with a scientific, professional, precise and efficient skin anti-aging solution.

   KANS encourages today's consumers to adopt a brave and persistent youthful attitude, and firmly stands by their side. With its professional anti-aging capabilities, it provides consumers with a youthful confidence!

KANS Founder

薇诺娜Winona

   Winona is a skincare brand founded by Yunnan Biotree Biotechnology Group Co., Ltd. in 2010. Its headquarters is located in Kunming City, Yunnan Province, and its founder is Guo Zhenyu. The brand focuses on the field of sensitive skin care. Based on dermatological theory and combined with active ingredients from Yunnan plants, it is produced in accordance with GMPC standards. The products include a series such as the Soothing and Moisturizing Special Care Cream.

   In 2019, it held the top market share in the domestic dermatological-grade skin care products market. In 2021, its parent company was listed on the Growth Enterprise Market of the Shenzhen Stock Exchange. In 2024, we will participate in the release of the "Clinical Diagnosis and Treatment Guidelines for Sensitive Skin in China", promoting the standardization of the industry.

Winona Founder
